Pin assignment for the

	R/C receiver / joystick port interface 


S1 (D-Sub 15p) 
-------------
1 to 1 connection to the IBM-PC Joystick Port


S2 (D-Sub 9p) 
-------------
Pin  1		Gnd
Pin  2		Gnd
Pin  3		+5 Volts
Pin  4		+5 Volts
Pin  5		
Pin  6		Throttle or trainer cord signal
Pin  7		Rudder
Pin  8		Elevator
Pin  9		Aileron
